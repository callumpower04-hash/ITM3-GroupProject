Key Takeaways

Remember these essential principles when evaluating media in the future:

  • Legitimate educators, executives, and public figures will never ask for credit card information or financial transfers in videos
  • Always verify unusual requests through official channels
  • Pay attention to visual inconsistencies like unnatural eye movements or poor lip-syncing
  • Consider the context - does the content match the person's known positions and behavior?
  • Use multiple verification sources before believing or sharing questionable content
